Jane, Jeffrey, and Kenna

COGS 187A

1. Visibility of System Status


"The system should always keep users informed about what is going on, through appropriate feedback within reasonable time."
– Jakob Nielsen



The language is primarily appropriate for the audience of students and parents. One exception is the use of the word "Bursar" instead of "fees/costs." The site even puts "(fees/costs)" in parentheses to clarify what a Bursar is. If there needs to be clarification, there is no reason not to use the simpler term in the first place.
