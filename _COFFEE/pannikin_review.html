Best drink ever! Tasty both iced or hot. Sometimes it is a bit too sweet, so if you aren't into sweet things you can ask for half the sugar.

Ellen P.

Better Buzz was another coffee shop I had on my list of coffee shops to try around San Diego ( a phase I'm going through if you read my other reviews). My friends are all obsessed with this place so I was excited to check it out. I tried the Best Drink Ever (I got it hot and my friend got it iced) and I also went a few other times to try the Hazelnut divinity and other flavored lattes.

Personally, I think it is way too sweet for me. It's more of a dessert drink than a coffee. I'm more of an americano type of girl so that may be the reason why I think it's too sweet. But, regardless, it did taste good but i don't think I will be going here too often as I like to taste the coffee more than the syrup. But, this place is worth a try!

Mel C.

Normally, I would consider a coffee stand like Better Buzz no different than Starbucks and a Coffee Bean. But because it's located in Fashion Valley and away from all the crowds, I would give Better Buzz a little more credit. If you're looking for that caffeine boost and do not want to wait in the long lines at the Starbucks upstairs, then Buzz is the place to be. If you've been to the Starbucks in Fashion Valley, then you know how much of a ruckus it is. It's small, service is hit or miss, and it takes forever to get your coffee. At Buzz, you can get your cup of Joe quicker, cheaper, and from employees that seem to care just a little bit more.

My choice of caffeine is usually an iced or hot Americano. Not a tough one for anyone to make, so this review is simply about the service, efficiency, and price. Better Buzz beats Fashion Valley's SB on all three.

PS: Like all other coffee shops, Buzz also carries a full list of flavored coffees, expresso's, and pastries.
