Torrey B.

Best coffee in San Diego hands down.. And I've tried pretty much all of the roasters in the county!

One thing, however, if you are reading this Bird Rock Coffee, the Latte's are often just luke warm!! Please heat the milk a bit more before pouring!!

Don't let that deter you from going here though.. Just ask for it extra hot!!

The greatest beans they have are the Makwa beans.. If you are buying beans, I highly suggest them!

Grace W.

I enjoy their coffee and will stop by if I'm in the area. Their coffee selection is very limited but they do a great job of making excellent, high-quality drip coffee and also their own take on lattes and cappuccinos. They have a lot of indoors and outdoors seating, but the indoors seating can get a bit dark.

There is a bit of a line on most weekends when I visited. A lot of regulars and locals.

Sireesha R.

What a cute coffee shop! Came here with a couple of friends on yet another perfect Sunday afternoon in San Diego. The baristas were very friendly and noticed when my friend wasn't feeling her drink. They remade it on the house! I got the iced mocha and they gave me a small cup to sample their pre-roasted coffee while I waited. So kind and thoughtful. My mocha was delicious and if I'm ever in the area again I'll definitely be back!
