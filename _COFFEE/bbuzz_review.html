Judd E.

This is the BEST coffee in San Diego, period. The downtown location is one of the best kept secrets in town. Go to the 3rd floor of the Wells Fargo building and check it out. The coffee is always fresh and hot and the staff is great. They always have 2 or 3 different choices of coffee and they switch them up daily.

Ron B.

I love the best drink ever, the white mocha, and the acai "smoothie"! I also love the inspirational quotes on the drive through daily. The only thing that sucks is the floor plan of the drive through. Cars get backed up on the busy street and arrive from two directions. There are a couple parking spots in the drive through line, but of course nobody ever parks there because your have to wait until its almost your turn to order to park, then getting out would be a nightmare. Maybe they should plow the front landscaping and have parking that is perpendicular to the street?

Olivia L.

Located on the 3rd floor of the Well's Fargo Building, this coffee shop makes for a convienient stop for caffeinie when you are tired of your company's brew. Luckily for me, all I have to do is head down the elevator, and I'm back at my desk in a few minutes. Coffee is good, service is fast, nothing to complain about.

One thing to note is that their bagels is about 35 cents more expensive than Starbucks bagles, and they didnt toast it for me either. Maybe you have to ask, or theres a toaster somewhere to do it yourself but that's something for you to find out when you stop by!
