Good coffee is hard to find. The search ends now.


Our team has found all the best coffee shops to visit around San Diego, and wants to share. Rather than the typical chain shops, these are the spots that offer something more. They're the spots that locals hang out, where addicts can get their fix, and brewing is an art. We present you with the important things to know about these shops, pictures to get you excited, and reviews about what the regulars say. Whether you're a college student during finals, rushing to work in the morning, or just want to relax, these shops are the place to go.
