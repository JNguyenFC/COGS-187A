To my surprise, this place is pretty cool. The coffee is amazing here, as they roast their coffee on site. The staff is friendly and very knowledgeable about their coffee. I ordered a hammerhead and it was very bold and tasted great.

Arlene I.

Best tasting coffee in the city by far. I rate it above Bird Rock and even the new, local, pour over, super hipster shops. The taste is consistent, the staff are wonderful and get to know their regulars even when it's busy. It DOES get busy BUT it's worth it. From reg drop coffee to a Viennese, Mex Mocha or Americano...all of the drinks are awesome. I even love the Caprese sandwich and other pastries.

Parking is easy to find in the early AM or late PM except weekends. North Park is "hip" now so whatever :)

Jennifer N.

This cafe is a little hidden off the main street, but it's definitely worth the trip. They have an adorable little patio, and the interior is made to look like a street in Italy.

I've only ever dropped by here for a quick bite, but the atmosphere is very cute and quaint. It's made to look as if you're standing out on the street - fake windows and fake plants and all that. Looks a bit tacky usually, but the cafe really makes it work.

They have great coffee, and it's really cute that they will draw a foam heart on your coffee. Also, probably one of the best chai lattes I've had in a while. The cafe is open most of the day, but there are some strange hours - you can only get pizza some of the time. Make sure to check the hours before you head in.

I'm bout that Mexican mocha life at caffe Calabria. I often feel great tasting coffee is wasted on me. My flavor palette hasn't refined itself enough for amazing coffee just yet. I'm also a huge tea drinker so my view is skewed. My coffee obsessed friends swear by it. They love it! I'm a bit more "main stream" coffee. Flavored mochas are usually my jam. The atmosphere is great. The people that work here are very nice and coffee knowledgable. It can be hard to get a table sometimes. It's a great place to sit and catch up with friends, study, or just sit alone like I am doing right now. :) enjoy!
