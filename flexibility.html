COGS 187A

7. Flexibility and Efficiency of Use

"Accelerators -- unseen by the novice user -- may often speed up the interaction for the expert user such that the system can cater to both inexperienced and experienced users. Allow users to tailor frequent actions."
– Jakob Nielsen



Although this website does contain a “home page” link on the top left corner, it is very minimalist and in a font that is not consistent with the rest of their brand. I think they could’ve made it better by having a single linkable brand photo on the top left consistent throughout the rest of the pages where you can easily go back to the home page without having to look for it.
